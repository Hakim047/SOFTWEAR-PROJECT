			Requirement of Stock Management System

	1: We need to have a ‘manager’ in the stock management system
                           Manager (Name, ID, Contact number, Email).

	2: We must have ‘inventory’ in the stock
	         Inventory (item name, invoice number, quantity).

	3: We have to have ‘Storage’ in the stock
	         Storage (unique Id, name, code, items).     

	4: We have to have ‘products’ in the stock
	         Product (name, unique id, barcode, description).

	5: We need to supplier in the stock
	         Supplier (name, date of supply, invoice No, item name, Reg No).

	6: We should be having shipment in the stock
	         Shipment(signature, date time, address, serial No).
